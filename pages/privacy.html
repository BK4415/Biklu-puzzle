🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Board Puzzle is designed to work entirely on your device and does not collect or transmit your personal data.

📂 Local Data Storage

The game stores the following information locally on your device using your browser's LocalStorage:

  • Game progress
  • Resume data
  • Selected game mode
  • Grid size
  • Theme preference
  • Game settings
  • Statistics and achievements

This information never leaves your device and is only used to improve your game experience.

🖼️ Custom Photos

Photos selected through the Upload Photo feature remain completely on your device. They are never uploaded, shared, or stored on any server.

🌐 Internet Connection

Board Puzzle does not require an internet connection for normal gameplay. No personal information is collected, transmitted, or shared while playing.

🔐 Data Security

Since all game data is stored locally, you have full control over it. Clearing your browser or application data may permanently remove saved progress and settings.

📄 Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated in future versions of the game. Any changes will take effect when the updated version is released.
