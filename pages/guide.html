🧩 Board Puzzle Guide

Welcome to Board Puzzle! Arrange the tiles into the correct order by sliding them into the empty space. Complete the puzzle using the fewest moves possible.

🎮 Game Modes

🔢 Number Puzzle

Select the puzzle pattern using the < > buttons.

  • Classic – Standard number arrangement.
  • Snake – Numbers follow a snake pattern.
  • Spiral – Numbers are arranged in a spiral.
  • Upside Down – Solve the puzzle in reverse orientation.

🖼️ Photo Puzzle

Solve beautiful image puzzles instead of numbers. Use the < > buttons to choose one of the five preset photos:

  • Raj
  • Ronit
  • Aradhya
  • Lovely
  • Biklu

You can also tap Upload Photo to create a puzzle from your own image.

📐 Grid Sizes

  • 3×3 – Easy
  • 4×4 – Medium
  • 5×5 – Hard

▶️ How to Play

  1. Select Number or Photo mode.
  2. Choose your preferred puzzle pattern or photo using the < > buttons.
  3. Select a grid size (3×3, 4×4 or 5×5).
  4. Press Play to start.
  5. Tap or swipe a tile adjacent to the empty space to move it.
  6. Arrange all tiles correctly to complete the puzzle.

🛠 Features

  • Shuffle – Randomizes the puzzle into a solvable layout.
  • Undo – Reverts your previous move.
  • Redo – Restores an undone move.
  • Resume – Continue your last unfinished game.
  • Show Numbers (👁) – Available in Photo Mode to toggle tile numbers On/Off.
  • Theme – Switch between Light and Dark mode.

💡 Tips

  • Start by solving the corners and edges.
  • Use Show Numbers while learning Photo Mode.
  • Use Undo if you make a mistake.
  • Practice on 3×3 before trying larger grids.
  • Fewer moves lead to a better performance.

Enjoy solving puzzles and have fun! 🎉
