🧩 About Board Puzzle

Board Puzzle is a modern sliding puzzle game designed for players of all ages. With multiple puzzle styles, customizable photo puzzles, and a clean wooden interface, the game provides a relaxing yet challenging experience that improves observation, memory, and logical thinking.

📋 Game Information

  • Game Name: Board Puzzle
  • Version: v1.0
  • Developer: Biklu Cyber
  • Release Year: 2026

✨ Features

  • 🔢 Number Puzzle with 4 puzzle layouts:
    • Classic
    • Snake
    • Spiral
    • Upside Down
  • 🖼️ Photo Puzzle with 5 preset photos:
    • Raj
    • Ronit
    • Aradhya
    • Lovely
    • Biklu
  • 📷 Upload your own custom photo.
  • 📐 Three grid sizes: 3×3, 4×4 and 5×5.
  • 🔀 Shuffle puzzles with guaranteed solvable layouts.
  • ↩ Undo and ↪ Redo support.
  • ▶ Resume unfinished games.
  • 👁 Toggle tile numbers in Photo Mode.
  • 🌙 Light & Dark theme support.
  • 🎮 Smooth animations and simple controls.

🎯 Our Goal

Board Puzzle is created to offer a simple, enjoyable, and brain-training experience. Whether you are solving classic number puzzles or your favorite photos, every puzzle is designed to be fun, relaxing, and rewarding.
